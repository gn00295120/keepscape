## Judging contract

The four criteria are equally important:

1. **Technological implementation** — deep, skilled Codex usage and non-trivial working code.
2. **Design** — a complete, coherent product experience rather than a proof of concept.
3. **Potential impact** — a credible solution for a specific real audience and problem.
4. **Quality of the idea** — creative differentiation and genuine domain understanding.

Every product milestone must leave visible evidence for at least one criterion. A feature that cannot be
shown or proven in the demo is lower priority than an end-to-end capability that can.
